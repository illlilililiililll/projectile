# 2024 Classical Dynamics

## Initial Condition
$$
\begin{align*}
& \begin{aligned}
&x(0) = y(0) = 0 \\
&\dot{x}(0) = v_0 \cos\theta = U \\
&\dot{y}(0) = v_0 \sin\theta = V 
\end{aligned} \\[10pt]
\end{align*} \\$$

## Equations
$$
\begin{align*}
& \begin{aligned}
&\dot{x}(t) = U e^{-bt} \\
&x(t) = \frac{U}{b}(1 - e^{-bt}) \\ \\
& \dot{y}(t) = \frac{bV+g}{b}\,e^{-bt}-\frac{g}{b} \\
& y(t) = \frac{bV + g}{b^2}\,(1-e^{-bt})-\frac{g}{b}t
\end{aligned}
\end{align*}
$$

## Terminal Time(T), Range(R)
$$
\begin{align*}
& \begin{aligned}
& T = \frac{2V}{g}+(\frac{1}{3}T^2-\frac{2V^2}{g^2})\,b \\
& R = U(T-\frac{1}{2}bT^2)
\end{aligned}
\end{align*}
$$
